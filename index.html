About Us

Nibergo Integrated Enterprise is a visionary agribusiness based in Western Kenya, dedicated to advancing sustainable and organic agriculture. Founded by Grace, we specialize in organic rice farming, milling, and packaging, while also offering eco-friendly farm inputs like natural fertilizers and organic pesticides.

We empower local communities—especially women and youth—through employment and training. Guided by the 13 principles of agroecology, our mission is to build regenerative, climate-smart farming systems that ensure food security and environmental sustainability.

🌿 Our Focus Areas

  • 💧 Sustainable Organic Rice Farming: High-quality rice grown with ecological integrity.
  • ♻️ Eco-Friendly Farm Inputs: Natural fertilizers and pesticides that enhance soil health and reduce chemical use.
  • 🌱 Agroecological Innovation: We apply all 13 agroecology principles to build biodiverse, self-sustaining farms.
  • 🤝 Community Empowerment & Training: Training programs that equip farmers with climate-smart techniques.

Programs & Partners
